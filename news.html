  • Bigs and Littles | Mar 19 2020

    Hi everyone! As the current students know, MHC will be going remote and some upperclass students were talking about the support we can provide to students who are not there yet. So if you want to be a Big or a Little, keep reading!
    For Bigs: In this trying time, our littles need more support than ever and it's time to step up. We don't know if there are going to be TA hours or something, so it's on us to make sure that even online people are still feeling supported and encouraged. If you have free time, internet access & are interested in helping out - fill out this form, tell your preferences and I'll do my best to connect you to those who aren't as far along in their CS journey as you are. https://tinyurl.com/mhc-cs-bigs
    For Littles: This is your chance to get yourself a big, who'll help you navigate the waters of cs and just will be there for you :) https://tinyurl.com/mhc-cs-littles
